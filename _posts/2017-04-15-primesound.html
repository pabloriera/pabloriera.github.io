Primes Sonification¶

Are prime number distributed randomly?...... Nope.

One way to see prime numbers is that they are a special case of the divisor function $\sigma_0(n)$, the function that counts how many divisor a number $n$ has. The prime numbers are where the function equals two. The following image contains first 20000 divisor function values for natural numbers from top left to bottom right. The value two is highlighted.
